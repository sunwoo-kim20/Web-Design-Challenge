Max Temperature


Our data suggests the existence of a strong quadratic relationship between latitude and max temperature: temperature is lowest near the poles and increases the closer one gets to the equator. One can also observe that cities in the northern hemisphere (latitude greater than 0) have, on average, lower temperatures than cities in the southern hemisphere.
